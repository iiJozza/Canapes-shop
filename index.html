What can you buy canapes for

After work

Hors-d'œuvre in French literally means "outside the work". Ironically, nothing beats charcuterie and crudités for business events, client meetings or after-works. With our seasonal menu and a wide range of cuts on the menu, there is always something new to look forward to.

Weddings

If you can think of anything more appropriate than canapés at a champagne reception, we'd love to know. Because we believe that canapes are perfect for weddings! Tasty and elegant bite-sized pieces that opens a whole new world of flavour.

Student parties

Forget about the usual snacks; let's take your celebrations to a whole new level. From savory bites that'll satisfy your late-night cravings to sweet treats that'll keep the energy flowing on the dance floor. Our canapés are here to make your student parties legendary. Get ready to munch, mingle, and make memories that'll last a lifetime. Because at our parties, the good times are bite-sized, and the fun never stops!
